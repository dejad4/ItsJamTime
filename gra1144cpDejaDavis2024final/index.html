Jamaican Beef Patty Recipe

Hello from one Foodie to another. Here is a recipe for Jamaican beef patties cooked from scratch. They are encased in a soft, flaky, and buttery exterior and stuffed with a delicious, well-seasoned beef interior. Filling is juicy and delicious, with a classic tasty crust. The ideal Jamaican quick meal to eat and enjoy. Ingredients and a step by step instructions are shown below.

Ingredients

Dough

  • All purpose Flour
  • Grated Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Jamacian Curry Powder
  • Tumeric Powder
  • Ice cold water

Filling

  • Scalions
  • Ground Beef
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Scottch Bonnet Pepper
  • Curry Powder
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Ginger
  • Jamacian Allspice
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients(flour, sugar, salt, curry powder, and turmeric powder). Combine the in the bowl with the shredded butter.Next, add the ice water and mix the dough until it comes together.
  2. Pour the dough out onto a sanitized surface and use your hands to knead it. Enough to bring the dough together into a single, cohesive mass.
  3. Chill the Dough for an hour
  4. Cook the ground beef till browned. After that, add the onion and saut√© it till soft. Stir in the ginger and garlic. Add the herbs and spices and continue to simmer. Add the butter, breadcrumbs, and stock after that.Set the finished filling aside to chill right after.
  5. After rolling out the dough, spoon part of the meat filling within. Form into a half moon shape, and then seal the seams with a fork.
  6. Bake in a 350° for 25 to 35 minutes
  7. Let it cool down before serving. Enjoy!
